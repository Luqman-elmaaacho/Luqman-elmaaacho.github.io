LOGIC GATES

It is an idealised model of computation of physical electronic devices implementing a boolean function, a logical operation performed on one or more binary inputs that produces a single binary output. Logic gates will make decisions based on a combination of digital signals coming from its inputs.
logic gates have two inputs and one output.
